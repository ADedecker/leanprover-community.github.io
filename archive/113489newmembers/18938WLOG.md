#### [ Mario Carneiro (Aug 14 2018 at 11:52)](https://leanprover.zulipchat.com/#narrow/stream/113489-new%20members/topic/WLOG/near/132101345):
<p><code>wlog</code> applies when the goal is symmetric under permutation of some variables, and there is a "symmetry breaking" relation that holds when disjoined over all permutations</p>

#### [ Mario Carneiro (Aug 14 2018 at 11:53)](https://leanprover.zulipchat.com/#narrow/stream/113489-new%20members/topic/WLOG/near/132101353):
<p>i.e. you want to prove <code>x * y &lt;= 1</code>, then wlog you can assume <code>x &lt;= y</code></p>
