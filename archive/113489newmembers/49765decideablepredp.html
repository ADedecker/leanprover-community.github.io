Bryan Gin-ge Chen (Apr 21 2020 at 17:03):

"An introduction to Lean" is very out of date. You may want to look at Theorem Proving in Lean instead. In particular, stuff like [_inst_1 : decidable_pred p] is explained in the chapter on Type Classes.

There are also some easier sources to get into Lean; have you seen the Natural Number Game?
